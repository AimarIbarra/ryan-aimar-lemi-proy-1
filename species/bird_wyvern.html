Kulu-Ya-Ku

Scratching Bird

Ovivore Outlaw

The Kulu-Ya-Ku is a medium-sized Bird Wyvern characterized by its prominent beak, adorned with vibrant earth-toned feathers. Its body is streamlined, with sharp talons on its feet and a short, sturdy tail that aids in balance. Known for its peculiar behavior of wielding rocks as weapons and shields, the Kulu-Ya-Ku's distinct crest on its head can be raised or lowered to reflect its mood, making it a visually striking and unique creature.

Pukei-Pukei

Poison Bewitching Bird

Gluttonous Gullet

The Pukei-Pukei is known for its colorful appearance and poisonous attacks. Its physical features include a vibrant and flamboyant color scheme, with a prominent, elongated beak reminiscent of a chameleon's. Its body is covered in smooth, iridescent scales, with notable wing membranes featuring striking patterns. The Pukei-Pukei's tail ends in a pronounced, venomous tip that it uses for both defense and offense, while its large, expressive eyes allow for keen observation of its surroundings. With a distinct frill around its neck and a generally sleek and agile build.
