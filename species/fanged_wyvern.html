Great Jagras

Thief Wyvern

The Great Jagras is a large, reptilian creature with a serpent-like body. Its most distinctive feature is its brightly colored, yellow-green scales with blue stripes running along its back. It has a long, slender tail that it can use as a weapon, sharp claws, and a massive, expandable gullet, which is one of its key characteristics.

Great Girros

Paralyzing Thief Wyvern

The Great Girros is a large, quadrupedal creature that resembles a grotesque fusion of a lizard and a snake. Its body is covered in pallid, sickly skin with patches of mottled scales. It has a long, sinuous body, short legs, and a hunched posture, giving it a distinctive and menacing appearance. The most striking feature of the Great Girros is its multiple rows of sharp, venomous fangs that line its jaw, and it has a habit of drooling poisonous saliva, giving its mouth a perpetually menacing look.

Tobi-Kadachi

Flying Thunder Wyvern

Flying Sparks

Tobi-Kadachi is a wyvern-like monster with a sleek, elongated body primarily covered in yellow fur-like scales. It has striking blue stripes and accents that adorn its back, tail, and limbs. The monster has a distinctive squirrel-like tail that is prehensile and serves both as a weapon and a balance aid. Tobi-Kadachi's head features sharp, jagged fangs and large, expressive eyes, giving it an overall agile and predatory appearance.
