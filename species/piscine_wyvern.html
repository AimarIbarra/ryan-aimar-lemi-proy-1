Jyuratodus

Mud Fish Wyvern

Sinister Swamp Shadow

The Jyuratodus is an aquatic monster known for its distinctive mud-coated body and eel-like appearance. It boasts a sleek and streamlined physique, designed for swift movements through both water and mud. Its scales are often concealed beneath a layer of thick, wet sludge, offering both protection and camouflage. Equipped with sharp fins and a powerful tail, the Jyuratodus is adept at swiftly navigating through its aquatic habitat, making it a challenging adversary for hunters to confront both on land and in water.
