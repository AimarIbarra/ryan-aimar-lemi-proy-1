Brute Wyverns

Barroth

Landslide Wyvern

Wasteland Warrior

The Barroth is a large, powerful brute wyvern, recognized for its massive size and distinctive mud-caked appearance. Its robust body is covered in hardened, rocky scales, with a layer of wet, compacted mud that provides both protection and added weight to its attacks. Its prominent head features a pair of formidable horns and a thick, armored crest that it uses to plow through the environment. The Barroth's forelimbs are particularly strong, enabling it to execute devastating charges and ground-slamming maneuvers. With a broad, muscular tail that it employs as a weapon, the Barroth is a formidable force, often leaving a trail of destruction in its wake.

Anjanath

Fierce Jaw Wyvern

Relentless Ruffian

The Anjanath is a formidable and imposing creature, reminiscent of a tyrannosaurus, known for its fiery breath and powerful build. It possesses bony protuberances on its head that serve both for display and defense. The wings and the nose are both retractable. Its reddish-brown scales, along with vibrant accents, contribute to its visually striking appearance. With muscular limbs and a strong tail, the Anjanath displays both agility and strength, solidifying its position as a dominant predator within its ecosystem.
